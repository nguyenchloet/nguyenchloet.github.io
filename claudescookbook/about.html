    My grandfather was raised on a farm in Selestat, France.
    He started working in kitchens at age 13 to be sure he had consistent meals.
    Later, he worked in restaurants in Paris, then immigrated to NYC and opened a restaurant called Nicola's.
    After selling the restaurant, my grandparents returned to France to retire and reside there today.
    These are his recipes taken verbatim by pencil and later stored online.

    In the future I would like to target the yield and ingredient amounts with JS so the yield can be manipulated and the ingredient amounts will adjust accordingly.

    Anything marked "Notes" are notes I added to help clarify instructions
