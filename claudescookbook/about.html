    My grandfather was raised on a farm in the Grand Est, Haut-Rhin region of France.
    He started working in kitchens at age 13 to be sure he had consistent meals.
    Later, he worked in restaurants in Paris, then immigrated to NYC and opened a restaurant called Nicola's.

    After selling the restaurant, my grandparents returned to France to retire and reside there today.
    These are his recipes taken verbatim with pencil and paper then tranferred to a database and slightly edited for clarity.

    Future upgrades: add translation widget allow conversions between # / grams output fractions instead of decimal value Anything marked "Notes" are comments I added to help clarify instructions
